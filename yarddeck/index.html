Overview

Problem

Tournament organizers struggled with:

  • Manual player registrations
  • Managing tournament waitlists
  • Tracking payments and confirmations
  • Updating player rankings manually
  • Sharing tournament updates through WhatsApp groups
  • Managing live tournament communication
  • Lack of a professional tournament platform

The Goal

The platform aimed to:

  • Centralize tournament registrations, rankings, payments, and live scoring
  • Improve tournament discovery and onboarding
  • Simplify organizer workflows
  • Create a professional digital tournament experience
  • Support multiple organizers through reusable platform infrastructure
  • Deliver a responsive mobile-first web experience
  • Improve communication and engagement during live tournaments

Outcome

  • 400+ event registrations across 3 live sports events
  • Centralized registrations, payments, rankings, and live updates
  • Reduced manual operational workload for organizers
  • Improved tournament participation and onboarding experience
  • Simplified waitlist and tournament state management
  • Enabled real-time scoring and tournament communication
  • Created reusable tournament infrastructure for multiple organizations
  • Delivered a scalable mobile-responsive sports platform experience

Solution

Centralized Tournament Platform

Designed a reusable tournament ecosystem that allows multiple sports organizations to manage tournaments through a single infrastructure.

Tournament Discovery

Created a streamlined browsing experience for tournaments, prize pools, formats, schedules, and locations.

Registration & Waitlist System

Designed simplified onboarding and registration workflows with OTP verification, skill-level selection, waitlist management, and automated confirmations.

Live Scoring & Match Updates

Built real-time tournament state experiences to improve player engagement during live competitions.

Rankings & Player Profiles

Designed player ranking systems and participation tracking to encourage long-term engagement and competitive progression.

Integrated Payments

Enabled seamless online tournament registration payments and automated confirmations using Cashfree.

Reflection

What Worked Well

  • Simplified registration flows improved participation
  • Mobile-first layouts improved accessibility
  • Tournament state systems reduced confusion
  • Integrated payments streamlined organizer operations
  • Strong sports branding improved engagement and community feel

Challenges & Learnings

  • Managing multiple tournament states within a clean interface
  • Balancing energetic visuals with functional usability
  • Designing reusable systems for different organizers
  • Maintaining clarity across information-heavy mobile screens

Key Outcome

  • Designed and shipped a responsive tournament platform for real events.
  • Created a reusable white-label structure for multiple sports organizers.
  • Integrated registration, waitlist, payment, ranking, and live update workflows into one experience.
  • Improved tournament operations by reducing manual coordination and payment verification.
  • Built a sports-focused visual system that supports clarity, momentum, and community engagement.

What I Learned

This project taught me that sports platforms need to balance clarity, speed, and excitement at the same time. I learned how strong information hierarchy and state-based UX can significantly improve tournament experiences, especially on mobile devices. Designing Yard Deck as a reusable platform instead of a single-event solution also showed me the long-term value of scalable systems and community-focused branding.
